WHAT'S NEW

All models are equipped with the A16 Bionic single-chip system, a 48-megapixel main camera and ProMotion displays with Ceramic Shield protection and a frequency range of 1-120 Hz, as well as accelerated LPDDR5 memory. The design of the line has been updated, there are two cutouts on the front panel of the smartphones.

All iPhone 14s in the US will be sold without a SIM card tray: the manufacturer says that improved eSIM technology will allow old electronic SIM cards to be transferred to the new smartphones.

The front camera (it has autofocus and recognizes the user faster in low light conditions) and the main camera sensor have been updated.

The iPhone 14 Pro has a new design - without the signature "bangs". A new color has also appeared in the range. The smartphone, unlike the "regular" 14th iPhones, has an A16 Bionic processor, which can perform 4 trillion photo operations per second.
